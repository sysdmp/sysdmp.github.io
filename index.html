Setting min = max requests an exact value.
A ÷ divisor forces the stat to a multiple of it (e.g. 100) and ignores the max.
Bias softly favors (+) or deprioritizes (−) a stat in the objective; it bends the build but never overrides a min/max.
Match ties a stat to another: = equal, ~ approximate (within 10; hp↔st within 100). hp and st only pair with each other; combat stats pair with any other combat stat.
Max! makes that one stat the top priority — it's maximized first, then the rest of your settings are honored as long as that maximum still holds (only one stat at a time; click again to clear).
